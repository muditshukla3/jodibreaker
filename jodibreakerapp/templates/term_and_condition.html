About the contest



Rewarding Jodi Contest is a Facebook contest brought to you by American Express. Between <date> and <date> ("Contest Period"), the contest will encourage users to select or create ‘Jodis’ on Facebook through the contest application, and get ‘Likes’ from friends. Contestants with highest ‘Likes’ on their ‘Jodis’ will stand a chance to win exciting prizes. Participation

Participation
To participate in the contest, you have to create your favorite ‘Jodi’ or chose from the existing list of ‘Jodis’. You can then ask your friends to ‘Like’ your Jodi. If you get the maximum number of ‘Likes’ on your ‘Jodi’ during the contest period, you stand a chance to win the contest.

Winning
The Eligible Contestant who gets the maximum ‘Likes’ on his/her ‘Jodi’ for the contest period, will be declared the winner.

At any point in the contest, you can check the trending Contestants and their ‘Like’ scores on the Leaderboard page.

Notification
All communication about the application will be published on the American Express Facebook page. Make sure you 'Like' the page to catch all the contest updates, tips and winner announcements on your Facebook Newsfeed.

Lucky winners will also be informed via the e-mail ID shared on Facebook.
